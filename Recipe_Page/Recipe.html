Mediterranean Cobb Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped salad greens (lettuce, spinach, or mixed greens).
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped.
  • 2 sliced crisp bacon, chopped.
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, sliced or chopped.
  • 1/2 avocado, diced.
  • 1 tablespoon chopped chives.
  • 1/4 cup blue cheese, crumbled.
  • 2 tablespoons red-wine vinaigrette dressing.

Steps

  1. Wash and chop all salad greens and tomato.
  2. Cook the bacon until crisp, then chop into small pieces.
  3. Slice or chop the hard-boiled eggs and dice the avocado.
  4. In a large salad bowl, combine salad greens, tomato, bacon, eggs, avocado, chives, and blue cheese.
  5. Drizzle red-wine vinaigrette over the salad and toss gently to coat.
  6. Serve immediately as a fresh and colorful salad.

Greek Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped tomatoes.
  • 1 cup chopped cucumbers.
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onion.
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled.
  • 1/4 cup olives (Kalamata or your choice).
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil.
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano.
  • Salt and pepper to taste.

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the tomatoes and cucumbers.
  2. Slice the red onion and crumble the feta cheese.
  3. In a large bowl, combine tomatoes, cucumbers, onion, feta, and olives.
  4. Drizzle olive oil over the salad.
  5. Sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper.
  6. Toss gently and serve fresh.

Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cups romaine lettuce, chopped.
  • 1/2 cup croutons.
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice.
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil.
  • 1 egg.
  • 1 clove garlic, minced.
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard.
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese.
  • Black pepper to taste.

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce.
  2. Prepare the dressing: whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, egg, minced garlic, Dijon mustard, and Parmesan cheese.
  3. Place lettuce in a large salad bowl and add croutons.
  4. Pour the dressing over the lettuce and toss gently to combine.
  5. Season with black pepper to taste.
  6. Serve immediately as a fresh Caesar salad.
